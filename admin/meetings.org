**** Antarctic Fast Ice presentation
Alex gave an hour long presentation and highlight of recently accepted paper
that looked at an 18 year period of satellite-mapped Fast Ice extent and novel
correlation techniques. These correlation techniques elucidate a statistical
categorisation of Fast Ice around Antarctica that appears to have physical
significance and be more aligned with trends than with previous, more
historical, geographical delineation of fast ice regional extent.

He also provided an overview of where my PhD will be focused on.
